Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about PickyPics and how it can help you pick the best photos

What is PickyPics and who is it for?
PickyPics is here for people who want to better select and sort photos by reducing the effects of decision fatigue in order to maintain decision quality.
Does PickPics collect my data?
No, our application runs without internet dependency, all your data is safe and secure.
Is PickPics available on platforms other than MacOS?
For now, PickPics is only being developed for MacOS.
